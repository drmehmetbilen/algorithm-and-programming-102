Greedy Checklist

Before trusting a greedy idea, identify the rule, the candidates, the constraint, and the risk.

1. Selection Rule

What looks best right now? Earliest finish, largest coin, or highest value/weight ratio?

2. Feasibility

Can we still add this choice without breaking the schedule, amount, or capacity limit?

3. Build the Answer

The final answer is formed step by step. The page shows the result growing after every decision.
