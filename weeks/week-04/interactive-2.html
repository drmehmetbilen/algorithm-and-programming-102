Shared Controls

These controls rebuild both simulations so the two algorithms are always compared on the same input.

How BFS Feels

BFS explores level by level. It is the natural choice when nearest nodes or shortest paths in unweighted graphs matter.

How DFS Feels

DFS goes deep first, then backtracks. It is useful for structure exploration, recursion thinking, and component analysis.
